CFX.Command.MaterialServices.MaterialTransport Namespace
The CFX.Command.MaterialServices.MaterialTransport namespace contains commands related to picking materials from stock areas, and transporting those materials to particular destinations within the factory environment.
Classes
  ClassDescription
RetrieveMaterialsRequest
Instructs a device (typically an automated stock / material storage and retrieval system) to retrieve a list of materials in the course of generating a material kit that will subsequently be delivered to a particular destination within the factory.
RetrieveMaterialsResponse
Response to the RetrieveMaterialsRequest command.
