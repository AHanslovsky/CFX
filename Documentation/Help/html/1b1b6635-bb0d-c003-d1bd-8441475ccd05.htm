CFX.Command.UnitIdentificationServices Namespace
The CFX.Command.UnitIdentificationServices namespace contains commands related to interaction with devices capable of resing and acquiring the unique identifier of production units in a factory (barcode readers, RFID readers, and other devices).
Classes
  ClassDescription
GetLastUnitIdentifierRequest
Requests the most recently acquired production unit identifier from a device. This allows a device or automated work center that does not have its own internal barcode or RFID reader to acquire unit identifiers from external devices (conveyor mounted scanning device, for example) prior to commencing work on a production unit (thereby allowing the work center to generate its own messages containing real unit identifiers during processing).
GetLastUnitIdentifierResponse
Response to the GetLastUnitIdentifierRequest command.
