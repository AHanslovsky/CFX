CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.SMTPlacement Namespace
Contains messages specific to work centers involved in the placement of SMT components on to printed circuit boards, known as automated pick and place equipment.
Classes
  ClassDescription
ComponentMisPick
An attempt to pick a component from its feeding device was not successful.
ComponentRecognitionError
Indicates that an error was encountered by the vision and/or component centering system after the component was picked, and installation of the component aborted.
ComponentsInstalled
Indicates that one or more electronic components were installed on to a particular production unit.
