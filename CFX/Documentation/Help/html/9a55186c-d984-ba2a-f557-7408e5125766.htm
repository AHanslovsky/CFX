UnitIdentifierRead Class
CFX.Events.UnitIdentificationServices Namespace
Contains messages related to the identification of production units for tracking and traceability purposes throughout the factory. It is indended for implementation by devices capable of reading the barcodes/RFID's/other of production units to acquire their unique identification.

UnitIdentifierRead
Indicates that the unique identifier of a production unit has been read by an identification capture device (barcode reader, RFID reader, etc.).
