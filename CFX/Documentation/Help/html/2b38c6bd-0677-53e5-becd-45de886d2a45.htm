CFX.Command.WorkCenterServices Namespace
The CFX.Command.WorkCenterServices namespace contains commands related to interaction with all work centers participating in a CFX mesh, regardless of their particular function. A work center is any location in the factory where production work is performed (via humans or via automation).
Classes
  ClassDescription
LockProcessingRequest
Instructs a work center to halt production. This might occur for a variety of reasons, such as an out of control process condition, an invalid material setup was detected, etc.
LockProcessingResponse
Response to the LockProcessingRequest command.
UnlockProcessingRequest
Instructs a work center that a previously issued LockProcessingRequest has been lifted, and production may resume at the work center.
UnlockProcessingResponse
Response to the UnlockProcessingRequest command.
