CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.THTInsertion Namespace
The CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.THTInsertion namespace contains messages specific to work centers that insert through-hole technology electronic components (THT) on to printed circuit boards (PCB's). This includes automated axial, radial, and DIP insertion equipment, as well as semi-automated (human guided) equipment.
