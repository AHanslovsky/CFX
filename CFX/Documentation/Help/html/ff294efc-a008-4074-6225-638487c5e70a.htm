CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.MaterialUsage Namespace
Contains messages that describe the raw materials and parts were consumed at a work center in the course of its processing. The materials that are consumed may or may not have been incorporated into production units processed at the work center.
Classes
  ClassDescription
MaterialsConsumed
This message is generated when a work center consumes raw material.
ToolingUsed
This message is generated whenever a work center uses a piece of tooling in the course of its operation.
