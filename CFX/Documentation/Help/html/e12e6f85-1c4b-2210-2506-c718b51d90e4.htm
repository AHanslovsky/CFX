CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.TestAndMeasurement Namespace
The CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.TestAndMeasurement namespace contains messages specific to work centers involved in the testing and inspection of production units. This include human inspection stations, AOI Equipment (Automated Optical Inspection), In-Circuit Test Equipment, Functional Test Equipment, etc.
Classes
  ClassDescription
UnitsInspected
Indicates that one or more production units were inspected at a workcenter.
UnitsTested
Indicates that one or more production units were tested on some manner (functional testing, electrical testing, etc.) at a work center.
