Namespaces
NamespaceDescription
CFX.Command
The CFX.Command namespace contains synchronous commands that must be implemented by all participants in a CFX Mesh.
CFX.Command.InformationServices
The CFX.Command.InformationServices namespace contains commands related to interaction with higher level level information systems running in the factory, such as ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) or MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems).
CFX.Command.MaterialServices.MaterialTransport
The CFX.Command.MaterialServices.MaterialTransport namespace contains commands related to picking materials from stock areas, and transporting those materials to particular destinations within the factory environment.
CFX.Command.UnitIdentificationServices
The CFX.Command.UnitIdentificationServices namespace contains commands related to interaction with devices capable of resing and acquiring the unique identifier of production units in a factory (barcode readers, RFID readers, and other devices).
CFX.Command.WorkCenterServices
The CFX.Command.WorkCenterServices namespace contains commands related to interaction with all work centers participating in a CFX mesh, regardless of their particular function. A work center is any location in the factory where production work is performed (via humans or via automation).
CFX.Events

Root CFX message topic. All endpoints participating in a CFX network must produce and process the messages contained within the root topic.

CFX.Events.InformationServices
Contains messages related to interaction with higher level level information systems running in the factory, such as ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) or MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems).
CFX.Events.InformationServices.QualityServices
Contains messages related performing quality related business processes within the factory environment, such as CAPA (Corrective Action, Preventative Action), FRACAS (Failure Reporting and Corrective Action), and MRB functions (Material Review Board).
CFX.Events.MaterialServices.MaterialManagement
Contains messages related to managing and controlling the inventory of raw materials within the factory.
CFX.Events.MaterialServices.MaterialStorage
Contains messages related to the storage of materials within the factory. For example, an automated (robotic) material storage and retrieval system might implement the messages of this namespace.
CFX.Events.MaterialServices.MaterialTransport
Contains messages related to picking and transportation of material kits (groupings of materials) to various destinations within the factory environment.
CFX.Events.UnitIdentificationServices
Contains messages related to the identification of production units for tracking and traceability purposes throughout the factory. It is indended for implementation by devices capable of reading the barcodes/RFID's/other of production units to acquire their unique identification.
CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices
Contains messages that are produced by any location in the factory where production work is perfomed. This includes but is not limited to, all automated process machines and equipment in a factory that perform a production related function, regardless of its individual capabilities or specific function. Also included are unautomated workcenters, where human operators perform production functions.
CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.EnvironmentalProcessing
Contains messages related to the environmental processing of production units, such as temperature stress tests, humidity chamber processing, bake processes, etc.
CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.MaterialTraceability
Contains messages that describe the raw materials and parts that were incorporated into production units processed at a work center.
CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.MaterialUsage
Contains messages that describe the raw materials and parts were consumed at a work center in the course of its processing. The materials that are consumed may or may not have been incorporated into production units processed at the work center.
CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.PackAndShip
Contains messages that describe the packing of production units and other materials into product packaging and/or containers for shipment.
CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.SMTPlacement
Contains messages specific to work centers involved in the placement of SMT components on to printed circuit boards, known as automated pick and place equipment.
CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.SMTReflow
Contains messages specific to work centers involved in the reflow of SMT components on printed circuit boards, known as reflow ovens.
CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.SMTSolderPasteDispensing
Contains messages specific to work centers involved in the printing or dispensing of solder paste on printed circuit boards, known as stencil printers and solder paste jet dispensers.
CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.TestAndMeasurement
Contains messages specific to work centers involved in the testing and inspection of production units. This include human inspection stations, AOI Equipment (Automated Optical Inspection), In-Circuit Test Equipment, Functional Test Equipment, etc.
CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.THTInsertion
Contains messages specific to work centers that insert through-hole technology electronic components (THT) on to printed circuit boards (PCB's). This includes automated axial, radial, and DIP insertion equipment, as well as semi-automated (human guided) equipment.
CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices.THTSoldering
Contains messages specific to work centers involved in the soldering of through-hole technology (THT) electronic components on to printed circuit boards (PCB's). This includes wave solder machines, as well as selective soldering equipment.
