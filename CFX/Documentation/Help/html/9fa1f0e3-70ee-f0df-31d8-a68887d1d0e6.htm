CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices Namespace
The CFX.Events.WorkCenterServices namespace contains messages that are produced by any location in the factory where production work is perfomed. This includes but is not limited to, all automated process machines and equipment in a factory that perform a production related function, regardless of its individual capabilities or specific function. Also included are unautomated workcenters, where human operators perform production functions.
